Disney World

Disney World is a popular vacation place for all things Disney. Located in Orlando, Florida, it's the perfect family get together. There are many different themed rides there that you immerse yourself in. There's something for everyone. If you like Star Wars, there's a land for you. If you like the classic princesses, go check them out in the parade. Disney World is also famous for its fireworks so before you head home after a long day of fun, watch the fireworks show at the Cinderella castle.
