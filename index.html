Meet Taylor Edwards

Taylor Edwards is a journalism student at the University of Maryland, College Park. She writes for the Black Explosion Newspaper, the university’s only Black-founded newspaper. There, she covers topics such as music, entertainment, and school-related events and issues. She also covers issues that impact minority communities. She’s had a passion for writing since elementary school and decided to write professionally after attending a journalism conference in high school. She learned that she could combine her love for pop culture and writing there. Outside of journalism, she rants about Marvel movies for hours and enjoys going to Barnes and Noble to add to her endless reading list.
